About Us

Real life Experience

Joshua, a software developer in Oklahoma came back to Africa in December, 2021 and decided to invest in real estate, unknown to him he was being swindled off his $250,000 savings by criminals. As the commercial real estate value keeps growing in Africa and investments being made in hundreds of millions of dollars, so is the increasing chances for investors, and buyers to get swindled.

Proptycheck leverages blockchain technology and data to improve the real estate industry in developing countries, anchored on a repository that enables investors, financial lenders, and real estate buyers, to check the status and authenticity of a property before payment, thereby saving them alot of time and money.

Proptycheck offers a robust system that will serve as a one-stop platform for everything real estate in Africa and other developing nations.

We believe that with our technology we can help investors and real estate buyers steer clear from collaterized, disputed, government marked properties, and land racketeerers. While improving commercial real estate - leasing, purchase and sales experience.

We are poised to seamlessly provide viable solutions to real estate challenges in Africa leveraging, blockchain technology, and data.

How does it work?

Financial lenders, legal & government entities can upload collateralized, disputed or government marked property, where users can run a status check on any property before making payment.

Mission

Our Mission is to save investors, buyers, and lenders time and money, and to help both the private and government sectors improve industry experience. 

Both corporate and individual users can leverage our platform to upload collateralized properties and to confirm same. This improves efficiency, saves time and resources, assuring convenience. 
For properties in dispute, legal houses can upload the said property on the platform to ensure buyers and investors are notified by a simple search option.

In partnership with the government, lands and properties are digitized (digital identity) which makes it easier to access on our platform. In turn real estates with such identity can serve as reliable collateral for accessing finance easily. Buyers would be aware of government marked areas and are shielded from any form of racketeering.

Owners, buyers and sellers can list their properties on our multiple listing service (MLS) platform, where actual buyers meet actual sellers. Our MLS makes it easier for a much more effective, transparent and coordinated real time validation, and sale/lease of any property.
